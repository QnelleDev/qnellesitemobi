Meet our team

Welcome to Qnelle, a forward-thinking company that challenges conventional ideas by rethinking traditional approaches using AI technology. Located in the city of Denver, Colorado, we concentrate on innovative AI-assisted solutions designed for small and midsize businesses. Our objective is to streamline your operations, enabling you to focus on what truly matters.

Meet AskBaptiste, our flagship product! It’s not your typical digital host; it’s like having a business-savvy trusted companion. Need to handle FAQs? Check. Taking orders and payments? Piece of cake! Making reservations? AskBaptiste has your back!

But wait, there’s more! our back-office functionality acts as a ticket aggregator, seamlessly pulling tickets from our platform, your POS, and third-party apps. Think of it as an efficient funnel that consolidates all your orders in one place with the ability to modulate the speed and flow with the touch of a button. Plus, brace yourself— because we have several exciting new features coming soon that will blow your mind!
